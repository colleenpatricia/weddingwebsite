  • Getting there
    • BY CAR
      If you are driving the postcode of the venue is DT6 6HG. There will be parking at the venue.
    • BY RAIL
      From London/ Southampton: Dorchester South station is on the line from London Waterloo to Weymouth. You will also be able to join this line from Clapham Junction, Woking, Southampton Airport Parkway and Southampton Central.From Dorchester South the venue is a 30 minute taxi ride, which should cost around £40.There are also bus services available (route X51 or national express) from Dorchester to Bridport, though these will take around an hour to reach Bridport.
      From Bristol:Dorchester West station is on the line from Bristol Temple Meads to Weymouth. From the station the venue is a 30 minute taxi ride, which should cost around £40.Leaving the train ay Yeovil will mean less time on the train but longer in a taxi...
    • BY AIR
      From London Gatwick:Take the train to Clapham Junction. From there, change onto the Weymouth service to Dorchester.From all other London airports: Make your way to London Waterloo station and take the Weymouth service to Dorchester. The venue is still another 30 minutes drive from the station, and, being the secluded rural countryside, is much more accessible / enjoyable if you have a car!
